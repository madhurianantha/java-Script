BILLY GRHAM

About

William Franklin Graham Jr. was an American evangelist and an ordained Southern Baptist minister who became well known internationally in the late 1940s. He was a prominent evangelical Christian figure, and according to a biographer, was "among the most influential Christian leaders" of the 20th century.

Books written by billy grham

BILLY GRAHAM wrote a total of 33 books, including many best-sellers and autobiographies, and many of his earlier works have been updated and reissued throughout the years. Here's a look at the highlights of his 70-year publishing career:
One of his first books, Peace With God, was published by Doubleday in 1953 and has sold over 2 million copies in 38 languages. It was revised and expanded multiple times including editions in 1984, 2000, and 2017.
His 1955 book, Freedom From the Seven Deadly Sins; The Secret Of Happiness was revised and expanded in 1985.
The 1965 book World Aflame was a New York Times and Times Magazine best-seller.
The Jesus Generation published in 1971 sold 200,000 copies in the first two weeks after publication. This has not been reissued, most likely because the content is dated, but if you can find a copy of the 1971 book it has the most amazing artwork on the cover.
